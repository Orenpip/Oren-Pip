About

I'm a first-year Aerospace Engineering student at the University of Virginia's School of Engineering & Applied Science, pursuing problems that sit at the edge of what's currently possible.

My work spans from NASA drone research to space station architectural design, machine learning competition models, and prosthetic limb development. I'm equally comfortable writing Python, modeling in Fusion 360, and presenting to a boardroom.

I grew up in Philadelphia, competed nationally in science fairs and coding challenges, and am now contributing to UVA's Solar Car Team, Rocketry Team, and CavaLimb prosthetics project simultaneously.

I'm interested in advanced propulsion, structural systems for long-duration spaceflight, and the application of AI to engineering design workflows.

Projects

Interstellar Gate
2025
Architectural Design

A lunar-orbit space station designed as an interplanetary exchange hub for the urbanized future of space. Features a non-rotating axial spine for microgravity docking and logistics, and a segmented habitation ring using the Dzhanibekov Effect — four moving masses that can flip the station's orientation on demand to reposition docking ports. Designed for multi-class simultaneous traffic, contamination-controlled crew transfer, and compact launch folding.

Blender 3D Space Architecture Orbital Mechanics Structural Design Dzhanibekov Effect
Cape Cod National Park Analysis
2026 · UVA × Deloitte
🥈 2nd Place

Evaluated five East Coast candidates for elevation to National Park status on behalf of Jefferson Strategy Group at UVA's Deloitte First Year Case Competition. Built a quantitative evaluation model using LightGBM and XGBoost (R² ≥ 0.94) to score candidates across economic impact, demand, national significance, and visitor experience. Recommended Cape Cod National Seashore — which generates $5.75B in economic output, 3.5× the next candidate, and supports 5,385 local jobs.

Python LightGBM XGBoost Data Analysis Strategy Consulting
NASA Search & Rescue Drone
Summer 2024 · NASA Ames Research Center
Published Research

As part of NASA's Aviation Concept Design Experience, designed a search-and-rescue drone optimized for storm-condition flight. Determined the optimal wing geometry for high-wind stability and integrated an AI pose estimation model alongside an AI voice-to-text model for autonomous victim detection.
